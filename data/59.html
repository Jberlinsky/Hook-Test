Our Objectives

We work with donors to increase the impact of their giving and, using our own resources, programs and activities, support thriving people and vibrant places with a strategic focus on:
  • Education to Career
  • Health and Wellness
  • Jobs and Housing
  • Community Safety
  • Civic and Cultural Vitality
Don't just give. Solve.

A certain kind of philanthropist comes to us. Generous but pragmatic. As with their business dollars, they hold their charitable dollars accountable. They expect an ROI, measured in human good achieved.

We welcome these men and women because, like them, we measure philanthropy by impact created, not just by dollars given. We use charitable asset management, grant making and policy leadership to drive systemic change, helping donors to not just tackle problems, but to solve them. What do you want to tackle next?
