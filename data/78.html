Starting out

Delivering great TV, brilliant broadband and award-winning customer service is no easy task. It takes a team of talented, ambitious and dedicated people working together to make it happen. People just like you.

Join us and you’ll play your part in keeping over 11 million customers entertained, excited and inspired every day. And because you’ll be helping us steer a course for the future of Sky, we’ll make sure you have everything you need to get on – whether that’s through giving you a great benefits package, the opportunity to try out new things or training from our team of top professionals.

The Sky Academy inspires young people, supports emerging talent and helps build skills and self-belief for future generations. It brings together a range of opportunities Sky delivers for young people, including all our Starting Out opportunities detailed on this website.

  • Graduates

    Are you looking for your next step after university? Make the future happen - build your career on one of our award-winning Graduate Programmes.

  • Apprenticeships

    Have you ever thought about an Apprenticeship? If you have your GCSEs, this is your chance to gain new skills in a permanent job, all while earning a real wage.

  • Placements

    Considering a year-out from university? About to leave school or college? Get ahead in your career - apply for one of our fantastic paid placements.

  • Work experience

    Want to get a taste of working life at Sky? Find out what it’s like and gain some valuable skills along the way with one of our Work Experience opportunities.
