Gecko Ridge

Located between turns 2 & 3, Gecko Ridge is perched on the outside of the backstretch at Chicagoland Speedway.  Each space provides the ultimate fan a panoramic view of the racing action over race weekend.

     

Gecko RV Park - Infield

Be in the middle of the action with a trackside or non-trackside Infield RV parking space.  Your Infield RV space gives you a 360 degree view of racing action during Chase weekend.

Infield electric camping is sold out. All of our other camping spots are going fast. Buy now!

       

GEICO Park Lower Ridge

Stay in Chicagoland Speedway's backstretch campground - GEICO Park Lower Ridge.  Complete with 30 amp electric, experience the NASCAR atmosphere in this RV campground.  For an incredible view of the on-track action, upgrade to Gecko Ridge.

       

GEICO Park Lakeside

Pitch a tent or camp from your RV in GEICO Park Lakeside.  If you want to watch the racing from the grandstands but stay on property, GEICO Park Lakeside is a great option.
