Company Overview

goBlue Labs combines mindfulness training with 21st century technology in order to help people improve their life and work. The foundational research that underlies goBlue’s technology was developed at Yale University in the lab of Dr. Judson Brewer. We will start by marketing a web/smartphone smoking cessation program to wellness programs that has been shown to be twice as effective as the leading quit smoking therapy.
