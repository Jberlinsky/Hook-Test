Open for business: Health Care HQ

Millions of American businesses and employees remain confused about the extraordinary changes unfolding in the nation's health insurance system. The National Restaurant Association is advocating for changes in the Affordable Care Act, helping our members understand it, and has created services and products to ease implementation challenges.

The NRA's Health Care HQ at Restaurant.org/Healthcare offers a one-stop shop for our members, including:

  • Knowledge Center – Get checklists, FAQs, deadlines and other details about how the health care law affects your restaurant company. Find out what you can do to get Congress to make changes in the law.     

  • Online Notification Tool – Use our free member-exclusive tool to notify employees of exchanges. Easily track employee notifications in your restaurant.

  • Marketplace (coming soon) – The NRA Marketplace will help restaurant employers shop for ACA-compliant health insurance more cost-effectively and efficiently.

  • Immigration

    Immigration reform: Outlook for 2014

    More than any other industry, the restaurant industry reflects the diversity of the nation. The National Restaurant Association supports immigration reform measures that meet the needs of U.S. employers by providing a clear path to permanent legal status for the nation’s 11 million undocumented workers, implementing a reliable employment verification system, and establishing improved border security that doesn’t inhibit legal travel and tourism.  Contact Congress today to urge action.
