  • About Seattle University

    Seattle University, founded in 1891, is a Jesuit Catholic university located on 50 acres in Seattle's Capitol Hill neighborhood. More than 7,400 students are enrolled in undergraduate and graduate programs within eight schools and colleges.

    In U.S. News & World Reports Best Colleges 2014, Seattle University continues to rank in the top 10 among universities that offer a full range of undergraduate and masters degrees in the West. The university has held a spot in the top 10 for more than a decade. See the full rankings.
