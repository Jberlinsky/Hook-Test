Why Choose Wisconsin?

The Wisconsin School of Business Ph.D. programs have a rich tradition of training scholars who can both enhance the intellectual understanding of business theory and practice and effectively transmit this knowledge to other scholars, business professionals, and students.

  • Our faculty are highly recognized for their expertise, cutting-edge research, and innovative teaching.
  • Our students develop close working relationships with faculty and peers that will pay dividends throughout their careers.
  • Our students become well-rounded scholars through pursuit of minor coursework offered by highly ranked departments across the University of Wisconsin-Madison campus.
  • Our graduates are leaders in research and teaching at top educational institutions around the world.

Ph.D. Information session--Is a Ph.D. in Business Right for Me?

Monday, April 7, 2014, 4:00-5:00pm

3190 Grainger Hall

Discover how your degree in economics, mathematics, engineering, computer science, business or the social and natural sciences could lead to an academic career in business.  The forum includes a faculty and student panel discussion and time for the audience to participate by asking questions of the panel.

Registration—email bheberling@bus.wisc.edu indicating your interest in attending.

Ph.D. On the Road

Are you considering an academic career? Attend a DocNet Forum to learn more about doctoral programs in business, including admission criteria, financial aid, and careers in business academia. These forums include a faculty and student panel discussion followed by a recruiting fair with over 25 DocNet member institutions. Wisconsin School of Business recruiters will be attending these events.
